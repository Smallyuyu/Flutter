## What is Widget?
Widget是Flutter畫面的基本單位，分為StatelessWidget、StatefulWidget<br>
* StatelessWidget：App執行後便不會做內容更動的Widget<br>
* StatefulWidget：能用來跟使用者互動的Widget

## Basic Widget
由Flutter列舉的Basic Widget共11種，如下：
* 1.  AppBar
* 2. Column
* 3. Row
* 4. Text
* 5. Icon
* 6. FlutterLogo
* 7. Image
* 8. Container
* 9. Scaffold
* 10. PlaceHolder
* 11. ElevatedButton
